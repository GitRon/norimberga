How Defense Works

Base Defense

Every building with a defense value contributes to your base defense. Build walls and defensive structures to increase this value.

Shape Bonus

Walls with exactly 2 neighbors (forming smooth lines or corners) receive a +5 bonus each. Build continuous, well-formed walls for maximum efficiency.

Spike Malus

Wall sections with 0-1 neighbors (spikes or isolated walls) receive a -10 penalty each. Avoid disconnected wall pieces and close all gaps.
