What are Threads?

Threads are potential threats to your city that arise when certain conditions are met. They represent vulnerabilities that could be exploited by enemies or lead to internal problems.

Prestige vs Defense

When your city's prestige exceeds its defense value, it becomes an attractive target. A wealthy city with weak defenses invites raiders and enemies.

Managing Threads

To deactivate a thread, address its root cause. For prestige-defense imbalance, either reduce prestige or increase defense until defense meets or exceeds prestige.
